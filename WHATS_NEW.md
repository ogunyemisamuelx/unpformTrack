## 🆕 Profile Avatars

**What it is:**
Staff members can now upload their profile picture!

**How it works:**
1. Staff sees their initial (first letter of name) in a circle by default
2. Click on the avatar to upload a photo
3. Choose camera (for selfie) or upload from gallery
4. Picture shows in round circle at top of profile
5. Admin sees everyone's avatars in the dashboard

**Benefits:**
- Easier to identify staff members
- More professional look
- Personal touch to profiles

---

## 📄 Paired Document Upload (Front + Back Together!)

**What changed:**
Before, you uploaded front and back separately. Now they're together!

**New Upload Flow:**

### Old Way (Before):
```
1. Upload image → Select "Front" → Submit
2. Upload image → Select "Back" → Submit
(Two separate documents)
```

### New Way (Now):
```
1. Click "Upload New Form"
2. Step 1: Capture/upload FRONT
3. Step 2: Capture/upload BACK
4. Review both sides
5. Submit ONCE - both go together!
(One paired document)
```

**Benefits:**
- Front and back always stay together
- Can't lose one half of a form
- Easier to review complete documents
- Better organization

```
└─────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Color Coding:**
- 🔵 **Blue border** = Front side
- 🔴 **Red border** = Back side

**Benefits:**
- See complete form at once
- No searching for matching sides
- Professional layout
- Easier to verify uploads

## 💡 Usage Tips

### For Staff:
1. **Upload avatar first** - Makes your profile look professional
2. **When uploading forms:**
   - Have both sides ready
   - Check preview before submitting
   - Can retake if needed
3. **Review your uploads** - Front and back should match

### For Admin (Your Uncle):
1. **Check staff avatars** - Know who's who at a glance
2. **Monitor document uploads** - Each count = complete form
3. **View staff profiles** - See all their paired documents
4. **Verify completeness** - Every form has both sides
